Epidemiological study “SPECTRUM”

The prevalence and antimicrobial susceptibility of circulating S. pneumoniae serotypes in adult population in Russia

Posted on 09 September 2021 Views: 0 Go to project: 0
Publication IACMAC MSD
Open

Study Objective

To estimate prevalence and antimicrobial susceptibility of circulated S. pneumoniae serotypes in adult population in different regions of the Russian Federation.

Dataset

  • Bacterial isolates: 500
  • S. pneumoniae: 500
  • Time: June 01, 2019 - January 15, 2020
  • Participating centers: 29 hospitals in 20 cities of Russian Federation

Inclusion Criteria

Isolates of S. pneumoniae obtained from patients with invasive pneumococcal disease (IPD), community-acquired pneumonia (CAP), sinusitis/acute otitis media (AOM) and healthy carriers.

Study Procedures

Re-identification, typing using real-time PCR with 27 primer pairs and antimicrobial susceptibility testing were performed in the central laboratory by standardized methods.

Study Coordinator
